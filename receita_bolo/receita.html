Churros Cake Recipe

Now you will learn how to make the famous churros cake full of cinnamon and the very traditional Doce de Leite straight from Minas Gerais.


Ingredients

Dough

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 spoon of baking soda
  • 3 sppons of butter
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 spoon of baking soda

Cover

  • Doce de Leite
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon powder

    • Utensils

    • Blender
    • Bowl
    • Wooden spoon
    • Cake tray

    Step by step

    1. Blend the egg whites until get stiff and reserve
    2. Using another bowl whisk the egg yolks with butter and sugar
    3. Add into this mix the flour and the milk always mixing it
    4. Put the cinnamon and baking powder into the mix
    5. Add the stiff egg white and mix it softly
    6. Put the whole mix into a greased tray and move it into the oven pre heated to 180 degrees
    7. Bake it until the top is golden or stick it to check if is properly baked
    8. Wait it to get less warm to unmold it
    9. Spred the Doce the leite on the top
    10. Sprinkle some sugar and cinnamon
